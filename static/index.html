Planet

If IPFS objects are floating in the space, this planet puts them together and makes them easy to find, pin and refer to. It can work as a personal (public) index for your files, a place people can find more about what is out there to be pinned or a simple way to navigate the interplanetary filesystem.

How to use

  1. You should have an IPFS daemon running on your local machine.
  2. Download binaries from fiatjaf/gravity-binaries and run gravity register thengravity put. Your stuff should appear here.
  3. Ask or tell me anything on #ipfs on Freenode or in the GitHub repository linked to in the bottom of this page.
